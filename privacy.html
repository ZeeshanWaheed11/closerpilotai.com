Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

Effective: January 12, 2025
Last Updated: January 12, 2025

Closer Pilot AI respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, products, and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, business information, and payment details when you sign up for our services.
  • Usage Information: Data on how you interact with our website, platform, and AI tools (e.g., IP address, browser type, pages visited).
  • Communication Data: Information you provide when contacting us, such as support requests or feedback.
  • Automated Data: Cookies, analytics, and tracking pixels to improve site performance and marketing.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • Process payments and deliver contracted services.
  • Send transactional communications (e.g., confirmations, reminders, account notifications).
  • Send marketing messages with your consent (e.g., offers, promotions, updates).
  • Improve our AI models and customer experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, hosting providers, analytics partners.
  • Business Transfers: If our company is acquired or merged.
  • Legal Obligations: When required to comply with laws or protect our rights.

4. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Opt out of marketing messages.
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Request data portability.

To exercise your rights, email us at micahdusseau@closerpilotai.com.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Retention of Data

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
